FAQ

Straight answers

Do you give buy/sell tips or signals?

No. Zyvora is an education academy — we teach you to analyse markets and manage risk yourself. We do not provide investment advice, stock tips, signals or portfolio management services.

Are you SEBI registered?

We are an education provider, not an investment adviser or research analyst service — so SEBI registration for advisory is not applicable to what we do. Our founder is NISM-certified (Reg. No. NISM-2510265038), and every program is strictly educational.

Will I become profitable after the course?

Nobody can honestly promise you profits — trading involves real risk of loss, and anyone who guarantees returns is misleading you. What we can promise is a professional process: structured analysis, risk management and the tools to develop your own edge.

What is the Zyvora Terminal?

Our in-house market platform — live dashboards, a searchable market-news archive, watchlists and pre-market alerts. It's included with Elite Mentorship and available to active members via Client Login.

Why isn't pricing listed on the website?

Because the right program depends on your experience and goals. Send a request and we'll share the full fee structure upfront in our first conversation — no pressure tactics, no fake discounts.
